RuPaul’s Drag Race is a reality competition where a cast of drag queens battle it out each week for the crown and the title of America’s Next Drag Superstar. The show began in 2009, and since then has grown into a global phenomenon with 17 seasons, multiple All Stars editions featuring fan-favorite, returning queens, and international spin-offs like Drag Race UK and Canada’s Drag Race. The queens face challenges that test everything from acting and comedy to sewing, singing, and performance skills. Each episode ends with a Lip Sync for Your Life, where the bottom two queens fight to stay in the race. RuPaul’s Drag Race has launched the careers of hundreds of queens and built one of the most passionate fan communities in the world. And now, on January 2nd, the show kicks off its 18th season, starring 14 new queens who are ready to fight it out for the crown.
