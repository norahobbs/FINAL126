RuPaul Charles was born on November 17th, 1960, in San Diego, California. RuPaul is best known as the queen of drag. In 1993, he released his debut single, “Supermodel (You Better Work)”, while living in New York City. The song launched his career, and sixteen years later, in 2009, the first season of RuPaul’s Drag Race premiered. Since then, RuPaul has built an extraordinarily successful career—not only as a drag queen, but also as an author, DJ, television host, comedian, and “supermodel of the world.”
